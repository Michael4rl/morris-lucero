PWP Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose

This project will be a professional biography, portfolio type website focused on driving new clientele to closings.

Audience

This website is designed with a prospect audience in mind, and is targeted toward individuals who are interested in buying or selling a home. Target audience to include: buyers, especially first time home buyers, real estate investors, house flippers, landlords, and property owners who wish to sell.

Goal

The goal for this website is to engage in communications with potential clients in regard to real estate transactions, whether assisting them in the buying OR selling of a property. Another goal is to give prospects the chance to familiarize themselves with whom they will potentially be employing.

Persona

Name

Amanda Collins

Profession

Customer Service Representative Supervisor at a major cell phone company. Just received a promotion and now is looking to secure a home with the newly-found finances. But Amanda has never bought a home before and is looking for a friendly, helpful agent to assist Amanda and her family with this intimidating major life event and future investment.

Technology

Power User of Apple devices. Since Amanda works at a major cell phone company and gets a 40% employee discount, not only on her cell phone bill but ALSO on all cell devices and accessories, Amanda has the latest Apple iPhone, iWatch, and iPad along with the best unlimited 5g data plan that her cell phone company provides.

Loves & Hates

Amanda loves social media and online interaction.

Amanda loves to be on her phone for hours throughout the day.

Amanda hates mean people

Amanda hates the long commute to work, maybe a home closer to work would be great

Amanda loves 2-story houses.

Amanda loves the idea of house hunting with her family.

BUT Amanda heard and hates that the home buying process is long and that it involves a bunch of paperwork which makes Amanda not want to start the process.

Amanda loves home decor

Attitudes & Behaviors

Since Amanda is on is often on the phone there are not missed emails, messages, or notifications. Amanda likes using google recommendations when searching for businesses and has been looking for Realtors in her local area online. Amanda wants to find a friendly helpful real estate agent who is willing to guide a first time homebuyer through this scary process. Amanda will not even look at realtors with a rating of less than 5 stars because they may not have a great attitude and Amanda hates mean people. Amanda wants someone who will make the process less stressful and quick as possible.

Goals

Amanda's goal is to find a Realtor that satisfies her expectations and to find a beautiful home that is just the right feel for Amanda and her family. Amanda will not settle for just ANY Realtor. Amanda is looking for a combination of the best but also a joy to work with. She expects the Realtor's professionalism and desires a realtor who will guide her and her family through the process as stress-free as possible. If Amanda feels a hint of deception or unprofessionalism, Amanda will not hesitate to find another Realtor.

User Story

As a power user of 5g devices, I need to find a reliable, enjoyable, and professional Realtor who will be the perfect candidate to assist me in finding the perfect home.
