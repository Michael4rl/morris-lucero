Content Strategy

Section 1

Section 1 will feature a background image with text overlay. Text overlay will say the name of the person and a small catchphrase relating to the person.

Section 2

Section 2 will feature a biography about the person with a headshot image of said person.

Section 3

Section 3 will feature awards, medals, or recognition that the person has received along with any images of said awards.

Section 4

Section 4 will feature a portfolio of the persons completed real estate transactions with a picture and information of each transaction.

Section 5

Section 5 will feature a contact form where consumers or prospects can fill out and submit the form.
